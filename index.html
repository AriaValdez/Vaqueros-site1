Welcome!

At Los Vaqueros Cafe and Grill we serve Tex-mex food in the heart of Kyle, TX. We have all your Tex-Mex cravings such as enchilada plates, tacos, fajitas ,and chimichangas. We also serve sandwiches, salads, and burgers. Come in to try any of our plates or create your own.

We also have two outside patios where we have live music and you can enjoy a variety of cold beer and mixed drinks. For event updates follow us on Instagram and Facebook.
